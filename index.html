Description

1. Goal
The goal of this tool is to aid you in exploring circadian rhythms in your data.

2. How it works
First, you will need to upload your data.
Currenly, we only support ".csv" or ".txt" files with the following format:

  • First column: Datime
  • Second column onwards: Variables for circadian analysis

Second, you will need to parse through a series of actograms of your data to select the individuals you actually want to analyze.

Currenly, the tool uses wavelet transforms to discover the periodicity of your data.
Optionally, once we obtain the period, the tool will apply multi-component cosinor analysis to obtain phase changes.
You can download the results for further analysis and plotting.
